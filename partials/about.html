// about

Based out of Fairbanks Alaska, I design, develop, build, and deploy instrumentation systems for arctic and subarctic climates.

At present I have an internship at the Alaska Department of Environmental Conservation where I perform weekly maintenence on regulatory instruments and am prototyping an in-house non-regulatory PM10 monitors.

Previously, my work has included developing and working with water depth sensors, All-sky systems, and Flux-gate magnetometers.
